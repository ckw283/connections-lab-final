leave a recipe <3 tell its story

what better way to connect with someone than with food? we all have memories with food and for a lot of us it's a tie to our family and our culture. some of us may have helped our grandparents bake snickerdoodles and apple pies in the kitchen, following recipes written on fading yellow index cards. others of us maybe didn't learn these generational and culture recipes as children and are asking our parents now how to make that curry, only to receive a somewhat frustrating answer of "no measurements, just add stuff until it's good". here is a place where we can document those snippets of childhood and share it with others so that those recipes (and stories that go with them) may live on!
